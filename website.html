Features

Listen with your favorite streaming service

There are a ton of awesome music streaming services out there. Sadly, most links on the web only point to Spotify. With Playify, you can open these links in either Google Play Music, Amazon Music, YouTube or the Google Play Music Desktop Player. All of this without any login.

Seamless GPMDP integration

Playify works best with the Google Play Music Desktop player, as it just plays the song instead of showing a Play Music search. If you are using Google Play Music, you should definitely using the GPMDP as well. More info

Playlists

Open a Spotify playlist and play them with the service of your favor. But not only that - if you are using Google Play Music, you can actually even completely transfer the playlist to your account to save it permanently. More info
